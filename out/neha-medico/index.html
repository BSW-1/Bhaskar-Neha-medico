How to Get Started

Three simple steps to place a wholesale order

We keep things straightforward - no complicated forms, no online portals. Just a phone call is all it takes.

1

Keep your PAN number ready

A valid business PAN number is mandatory for all wholesale accounts. This is a legal requirement for pharmaceutical distribution.

2

Call us directly

Reach out to our wholesale desk at 9842845688 or 9829786509. Share your PAN details and tell us what you need.

3

We handle the rest

Our team will set up your account, confirm pricing, and arrange delivery. All further business is conducted over phone - no online ordering.
